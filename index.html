The Platform

Built on six pillars

🎨
AI Studio
Generate images in Kente, Ndebele, and Adinkra styles. Write in Setswana, Swahili, Yoruba. Create music in Afrobeats and Amapiano.
🎬
Creator Suite
End-to-end production tools. Write, design, record, edit, and publish — all in one place built for African creators.
🌍
Cultural Engine
The intelligence layer. A knowledge base of 54 African countries, 500+ languages, and millennia of art and storytelling.
💰
Creator Economy
Mobile money first. Orange Money, M-Pesa, MTN MoMo. Sell your work. Earn in your currency. PPP-adjusted pricing.
🤝
Community
African creators finding each other, collaborating, and building movements across the continent and diaspora.
📡
Distribution
Publish everywhere from one place — YouTube, TikTok, Boomplay, Audiomack, ShowMax, and beyond.
